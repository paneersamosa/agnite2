Our Goal
We are agnite, a nonprofit with the goal of spreading cultural and technological ties between students in the United States and college-level students in India through hands-on arduino-based projects. Through Agnite, we aim to provide application based knowledge as well as collaboration, teaching experience, as well as international communication experience for students in both the United States and India.
